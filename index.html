About Safari Travels

Safari Travels is a proudly African tour company with roots in Kenya and a passion for showcasing the continent’s rich diversity. For over five years, we’ve curated unforgettable journeys that celebrate Africa’s landscapes, cultures, and people — from the savannahs of East Africa to the deserts of the North and the coastlines of the South.

What sets us apart is our on-the-ground expertise, handpicked experiences across multiple African destinations, and our commitment to sustainable tourism. Each itinerary is crafted to deliver more than just sightseeing — we offer authentic connections, cultural depth, and meaningful impact. Whether you're exploring hidden waterfalls in Kenya, spotting wildlife in South Africa, or trekking through Morocco’s Atlas Mountains, Safari Travels ensures your African adventure is as unique as you are.
